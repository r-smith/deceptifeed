Threat Feed

The threat feed lists IP addresses that have interacted with Deceptifeed's honeypots. Its goal is to help you build an automated defense system.

View the threat feed from your browser, or see the documentation for other ways to access the data.

Supported formats include:

  • Plain text is the most commonly used format for integration with firewalls.
  • JSON and CSV formats offer flexibility for integrating with various tools and workflows.
  • TAXII is designed for integration with Threat Intelligence Platforms (TIPs).
